About Me

A brief introduction to who I am and what I do.

Hello! I'm Chase, a problem-solver who thrives under pressure, driven by a simple philosophy: never stop learning, and always leave a system better and more secure than I found it.

Serving as a Network Communication Systems Specialist and NCO in the U.S. Army, I’ve managed everything from fiber optics and computer networking to satellite and radio hardware. My technical foundation is backed by CompTIA (Network+, Security+), ETA International, and LPI Linux Essentials certifications. However, my greatest takeaways from the military are my leadership and decision-making skills. Managing personnel and mission-critical systems in high-pressure environments taught me how to lead with empathy and execute with precision.

Today, my passion lies in cybersecurity and software engineering. I am transitioning my military expertise into a Network Administrator role, where I can continuously learn and build innovative, secure solutions. I am excited about the future of tech—let's connect and secure our digital future together..
