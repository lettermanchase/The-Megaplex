ℹ️ HOW WAGERS WORK

Read this before you challenge!
⚔️ The Basics
  • Both players bet the same amount of tokens.
  • You and your opponent each play the chosen game.
  • Highest score (or lowest, for time-based games) wins the pot.
  • You have 24 hours after the wager is accepted to play and submit.
🎯 Score Rules — IMPORTANT
  • Only the score you achieve during the wager counts. When you open the game, your wager "starts fresh" from zero.
  • For idle/accumulator games like Clicker Frenzy, only the cosmic energy you earn during this session is submitted — not your lifetime total. This keeps wagers fair between veterans and new players.
  • Your real lifetime progress and all-time high score are never affected by a wager.
  • You must submit your score from the Friends page after playing.
🏆 Payouts
  • Winner: Takes the full pot minus a 10% house cut.
  • Tie: Both players are refunded their bet minus a 5% cut each.
  • No-show / expired: Player who didn't submit forfeits the wager.
  • You must click "CLAIM" on the friends page to collect your winnings.
⛔ Other Rules
  • You can only wager friends — send a friend request first.
  • You can have multiple wagers active at the same time.
  • Tokens are escrowed (held) the moment you challenge or accept.
  • Cancelling a pending challenge refunds you immediately.
